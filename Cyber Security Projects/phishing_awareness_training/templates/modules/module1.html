Module 1: Recognizing Phishing Emails

Common Characteristics

  • Generic Greetings: "Dear Customer" instead of your name
  • Urgent Language: Creates false sense of urgency
  • Suspicious Sender Addresses: Misspelled company names
  • Poor Grammar/Spelling: Unprofessional writing
  • Unexpected Attachments: Files you didn't request
