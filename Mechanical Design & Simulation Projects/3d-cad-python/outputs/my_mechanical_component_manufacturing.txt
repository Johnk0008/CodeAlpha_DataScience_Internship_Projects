MANUFACTURING INSTRUCTIONS
==================================================

SPUR GEAR MANUFACTURING:
Material: Steel AISI 4140
Number of teeth: 24
Module: 2.5 mm
Pitch diameter: 60.0 mm
Outer diameter: 65.0 mm
Bore diameter: 12 mm
Thickness: 15 mm

Manufacturing Steps:
1. Cut gear blank from Steel AISI 4140 stock
2. Rough turn to outer diameter
3. Bore center hole to size
4. Cut teeth using gear hobbing machine
5. Heat treatment: Carburize and harden to 55-60 HRC
6. Grind bore to final size
7. Deburr and inspect

Quality Requirements:
- AGMA Quality Class 8 or better
- Tooth profile tolerance: ±0.02 mm
- Runout: < 0.05 mm
- Surface finish: 0.8 μm Ra on teeth

BEARING HOUSING MANUFACTURING:
Material: Cast Iron
Outer diameter: 65 mm
Bore diameter: 35 mm
Width: 30 mm

Manufacturing Steps:
1. Cast housing to near-net shape
2. Rough machine all surfaces
3. Finish bore to H7 tolerance
4. Machine mounting surfaces
5. Drill and tap bolt holes
6. Deburr and clean

Quality Requirements:
- Bore tolerance: H7
- Surface finish: 1.6 μm Ra in bore
- Concentricity: 0.03 mm

GENERAL NOTES:
- All dimensions in millimeters
- Apply anti-corrosion coating after machining
- Use appropriate cutting fluids during machining
- Follow all safety procedures
